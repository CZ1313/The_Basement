About Us

With a passion for art and imagination, we hold an ambition to present a stage where any "unknown" artist can present their work. We here at strive for good quality products that deliver comfort along with fresh new style.

And what better way to do that than displaying amazing artwork from the yet to be discovered, fresh off the page or have been at it for a while, "unknown" artist

And what we mean by "unknown" is that we are talking about the ones who have not had the exposure or opportuntiy to showcase their work. The ones who grind it out everyday! The ones who you pass by on the street, at work, in a store -- probably at a Michaels or any store that carries art supplies-- your friend, relative, neighbor, the ones at the kitchen table, in their room, or in the basment, ----laying out, only what their imagination creates!
